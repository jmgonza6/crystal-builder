19  * Left mouse rotate, zoom

21  *
22  * Keyboard events:
23  * 'A' display atoms and unit cell
24  * 'a' display atoms only
25  * 'B' display bonds, uniform nearest neighbor cutoff
26  * 'b' hide bonds
27  * 'C' increase nearest neighbor cutoff
28  * 'c' decrease nearest neighbor cutoff
29  * 'e' exit and save the data from the rendering scene
30  * 'o' orthographic projection of the crystal
31  * 'p' perspective projection of the crystal
32  * 'r' then LeftButton rotate the crystal in all 3 dimensions
33  * 'W' change background to white and bounding box to black
34  * 'w' change background to black and bounding box to white
35  * 'z' then LeftButton zoom toward/away from the crystal
36  * '0' reset orientation of crystal to looking down z toward xy plane
37  * '1' view the xz "a-c" plane of the crystal
38  * '2' view the yz "b-c" plane of the crystal
39  * '3' rotate crystal about x by -45˚, and then -45˚ about z, 'psuedo' perspective view
40  * '+' increase the particle radius uniformly
41  * '-' decrease the particle radius uniformly
42  *
43  * Up arrow Shift the crystal in positive vertical screen direction
44  * Down arrow Shift the crystal in negative vertical screen direction
45  * Left arrow Shift the crystal in negative horizontal screen direction
46  * Right arrow Shift the crystal in positive horizontal screen direction
47  *
48  \endverbatim
